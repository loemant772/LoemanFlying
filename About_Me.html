About Me

My name is Triston Loeman, I am 25 years old
and I am most likely your flight instructor. I
grew up in Hamilton Ontario. After graduating
highschool I moved to Ottawa to pursue
my aviation career.

I studied Aviation Management at Algonquin College.
While studying in Ottawa I worked as a Customer
Service Representative for Porter Airlines.

I have been working as a Flight Instructor since completing
my Instructor rating at the end of 2020. If you have any
questions or concerns regarding training, always feel free to reach me at
LoemanFlying@gmail.com.
