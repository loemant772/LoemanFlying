About Me

My name is Triston Loeman, I am 24 years old
and I am most likely your flight instructor. I
grew up and lived in Hamilton Ontario. After
graduating highschool I moved to Ottawa to persure
my aviation career.

I studied Aviation Management in Ottawa Ontario
at Algonquin College. While studying in Ottawa I worked
as a Customer Service Representative for Porter Airlines.

With the recent events over the past few years I returned
to my home in Hamilton Ontario where I completed my
Flight Instructor Rating and have been working as a Flight Instructor
since the end of 2020.
