About Me

My name is Triston Loeman, I am 24 years old and I am most likely your flight instructor.

I grew up in Hamilton Ontario. After graduating from highschool I moved to Ottawa Ontario where I studied Aviation Management at Algonquin College. While studying in Ottawa I worked as a Customer Service Representative for Porter Airlines.

With the recent events over the past few years I returned to my home in Hamilton Ontario where I completed my Flight Instructor Rating and have been working as a Flight Instructor since the end of 2020.
